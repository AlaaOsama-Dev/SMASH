Mobility

Over time poor movement, injury, and stationary positions cause our muscles to change and shorten, often resulting in decreased mobility. In order to unlock Yourself and move freely, you need to be able to move through a full range of motion and create stability in end-range positions. Our mobility class combines soft tissue work with static stretching and stability work to unlock range of motion that has been lost over time and improve positioning.
Class takes place in the STUDIO
Mandatory: Fitness Mat, small towel
